Companion Levels Benefits

One-Time Trip

  • One trip to wherever the Doctor's next planned trip is
  • No fee

Week-Long Trip

  • Spend one week with the Doctor
  • Mostly planned trips
  • May get to choose an additional location to go to
  • Fee: $20

Month-Long Trip

  • Spend a month traveling with the Doctor
  • Will go on planned trips
  • Get to choose locations to visit when no trips are planned
  • Fee: $70

Year-Long Trip

  • Spend an entire year traveling with the Doctor
  • Help plan trips for the lower tier companions
  • Plan trips for just you and the Doctor
  • Chance to be in a spotlight position
  • Fee: $200
