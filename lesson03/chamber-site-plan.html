TARDIS Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Overview

Site Name

TARDIS Chamber of Commerce. This name respresents the base of operations for the chamber.

Purpose

The TARDIS Chamber of Commerce shows what the Doctor has done recently and what he is planning to do next in order to attract new companions for the Doctor. The chamber also shows some of the features of the TARDIS as a bonus for future companions.

Scenarios

  • What historical events is the Doctor going to in the next few months?
  • What can I do in the TARDIS?
  • How do I join the Doctor as a companion?
